Founder Questions

Honestly answered.

This is a group workshop designed to produce startup-specific outcomes. You work on your own business context throughout the session. The facilitation is built around your stage, team size, and growth goals, not a one-size-fits-all template.
Facilitators guide each founder through self-discovery, shared planning, and 1:1 feedback within 90 minutes. Org design is not a one-time exercise. Like gardening, it requires ongoing pruning and adaptation. This session gives you the tools and clarity to keep doing that well.
Founders navigating the $0.5M to $10M journey, or the equivalent headcount journey from 20 to 100 employees. If you are in that window, or approaching it, this session was built for you.
Groups are structured based on funding stage (bootstrapped, funded, or currently raising), current headcount, and industry focus. The goal is to cross-pollinate learning among founders with compatible organisational journeys, not to cluster identical companies together.
